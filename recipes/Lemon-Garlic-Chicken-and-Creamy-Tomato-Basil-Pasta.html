Lemon Garlic Chicken and Creamy-Tomato-Basil-Pasta

Description: This one-pan wonder combines juicy lemon garlic chicken, sun-kissed cherry tomatoes, creamy tomato basil sauce, and perfectly al dente pasta for a weeknight dinner that's easy to prepare and bursting with flavor. Perfect for: Weeknight dinners, family meals, potlucks, and anyone who loves a flavorful and convenient meal. Serving size: 2-3 servings Time commitment: 40 minutes total (20 minutes prep, 20 minutes cooking)

Ingredients:

  1. 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  2. 1 tablespoon olive oil
  3. 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  4. 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  5. 1/4 teaspoon salt
  6. 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  7. 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  8. 1 cup chicken broth
  9. 1/2 cup heavy cream
  10. 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  11. 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  12. 1/2 cup dry pasta (e.g., penne, farfalle, rotini)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large bowl, toss the chicken breasts with olive oil, oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Arrange the chicken breasts in a single layer on a large baking sheet.
  3. Scatter the cherry tomatoes around the chicken.
  4. Pour the chicken broth into the bottom of the baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the tomatoes are softened.
  6. Remove from the oven and stir in the heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, and fresh basil.
  7. Add the dry pasta to the baking sheet and toss to coat in the sauce.
  8. Bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the pasta is cooked through and al dente.
  9. Serve immediately, garnished with additional fresh basil if desired.
