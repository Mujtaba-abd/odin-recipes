Salmon with Honey Garlic Glaze

Description: This flavorful dish features perfectly cooked salmon glazed with a sweet and savory honey garlic sauce. The ginger and soy sauce add depth of flavor, while the sesame seeds provide a delightful crunch. Perfect for: Special occasions, date nights, or any time you want a delicious and elegant meal. Serving size: 2 servings Time commitment: 25 minutes total (10 minutes prep, 15 minutes cooking

Ingredients:

  • 2 salmon fillets (4-6 oz each)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds

Instructions:

  • Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper
  • In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, honey, garlic, soy sauce, ginger, and red pepper flakes (if using).
  • Place the salmon fillets on the prepared baking sheet.
  • Brush the salmon with half of the honey garlic glaze.
  • Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the salmon is just cooked through.
  • Brush the salmon with the remaining glaze and sprinkle with sesame seeds.
  • Bake for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the glaze is golden brown and bubbly.
  • Serve immediately, with rice, vegetables, or your favorite side dishes.
